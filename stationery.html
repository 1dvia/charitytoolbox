Full list of stationery that we accept:

  • Stationery:
    • Pencil cases
    • Pencils
    • Erasers
    • Rulers
    • Pens
    • Correction tapes
    • Sharpeners
    • Notebooks
    • Paper
  • Others:
    • Motivational notes
    • Books

Terms & Conditions

  • All donations (except for books) must be new and unused. Books can be used but they must be in good condition.
  • Masks that are donated must be in their plastic covers.
  • All valid donations will go to students of underprivileged families.
